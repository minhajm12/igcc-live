Reports We Provide

Diamond Report

IGI offers diamond reports in multiple formats to cater to global markets, adhering to strict international standards and security measures. Each diamond undergoes state-of-the-art screening to determine if it's naturally mined, lab-grown, or simulant. Experienced gemologists perform detailed assessments, noting any permanent treatments, while non-permanent treatments are excluded from reports. An IGI Laboratory Report is a trusted symbol of quality and confidence in the gemological industry..

  • Global Acceptance:Recognized worldwide as a standard of trust.
  • Comprehensive Analysis:Includes detailed gemological characteristics.
  • Strict Standards:Adheres to rigorous international guidelines.
  • Security Measures:Incorporates advanced security features to prevent tampering.

Jewellery Report

IGI provides detailed Jewellery Reports to ensure the highest standards of quality and authenticity for fine jewelry pieces. Each piece is meticulously examined by skilled gemologists to assess its craftsmanship, materials, and overall value. The report includes comprehensive details about the metals, gemstones, and any special features of the jewelry. IGI's Jewellery Report is a mark of excellence and reliability in the jewelry industry.

  • Global Acceptance: A recognized standard of trust in the international jewelry market.
  • Comprehensive Analysis:Detailed information on the craftsmanship, materials, and features of the jewelry.
  • Strict Standards: Follows rigorous international guidelines for jewelry assessment.
  • Security Measures:Equipped with advanced security features to ensure the report's integrity.

Color Stone Report

IGI's Color Stone Reports provide an in-depth analysis of colored gemstones, ensuring their quality and authenticity. Each stone undergoes thorough evaluation by expert gemologists to determine its natural characteristics, including color, clarity, cut, and origin. The report highlights any treatments the stone may have undergone, whether permanent or temporary. An IGI Color Stone Report stands as a trusted reference for color stone valuation.

  • Global Acceptance:Widely recognized as a benchmark of trust in the global gemological community.
  • Comprehensive Analysis: Includes detailed gemological data on color, clarity, and other important attributes.
  • Strict Standards:Adheres to stringent international standards for color stone evaluation.
  • Security Measures:Features advanced security protocols to safeguard the authenticity of the report.
