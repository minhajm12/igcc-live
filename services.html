Our Services

What We Offer

Diamond Screening

Diamond screening is a crucial process used to identify and separate natural diamonds from synthetic or treated stones. Using advanced technology like spectroscopy and X-ray fluorescence, IGCC ensures that only genuine, natural diamonds are certified. This process is essential for maintaining trust and transparency in the diamond industry, providing consumers with confidence in their purchases.

Diamond Sorting

Diamond sorting involves categorizing diamonds based on various factors such as size, shape, color, and clarity. This meticulous process ensures that diamonds are properly graded and matched for quality, making it easier for buyers to select stones that meet their specific requirements. IGCC’s expert sorting services guarantee precision and consistency in every diamond selection.

Diamond Security Seal

The Diamond Security Seal is a unique, tamper-evident packaging that ensures the authenticity and integrity of your certified diamond. Each sealed diamond is securely encased with its corresponding certification, protecting it from tampering and providing buyers with peace of mind. This seal is an essential safeguard in maintaining the diamond’s value and authenticity throughout the supply chain.

Diamond Laserscribe

Diamond Laserscribe is a precision laser inscription service that allows unique identifiers, such as a certification number or custom message, to be engraved on the diamond’s girdle. This microscopic inscription is invisible to the naked eye but easily verifiable under magnification, providing a permanent, traceable mark that ensures the diamond’s identity and authenticity without affecting its appearance.
